## Sponsor tiers (priority support)
Support is best-effort. We do not guarantee fixed response times, but sponsors get priority handling.

### $20+/month — Priority issues
- Issues tagged **sponsor-priority**
- Faster triage and prioritization
- You can request small improvements / fixes

### $100+/month — Direct contact
- Priority issues + roadmap discussion
- Direct contact channel (e.g. Discord/Telegram/email)
- Calls for explanation, debugging sessions, feature planning (reasonable limits)
